Maheshwar Boat Upgradation Project

Project Overview

  • Funding: CSR initiative by Warehousing Corporation
  • Total CSR Grant: ₹60 lakhs
  • Objective: Upgrade traditional boats with fuel-efficient, low-emission outboard engines

Project Highlights

  • Engine Type: 9.9 HP Yamaha Petrol Outboard Engines
  • Procurement Platform: GeM (Government e-Marketplace)
  • Engine Cost: ₹1,41,000 + 28% GST
  • Total Units: 30 engines (25 initially + 5 additional approved)

Phased Implementation Plan

  • Stage 1: Prototype (1 engine) ✅
  • Stage 2: 10 engines ✅
  • Stage 3: 7 engines ✅ (Panchnama & invoices received)
  • Stage 4: 12 engines (7 + 5) ⏳ Work order to be issued

Bar Chart

Progress Update

  • Total Engines Installed: 18
  • Remaining: 12
  • Installation Status: Ongoing

Expected Outcomes

  • Safer and more reliable boat operations
  • Lower emissions and better fuel efficiency
  • Boost to eco-friendly river tourism
  • Enhanced livelihood for boat operators

Government Coordination & Support

  • Active coordination with district authorities
  • Timely approvals and inspections
  • Support for operator training and implementation
